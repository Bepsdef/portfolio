400g

Brief

Design a photographic book about Tabuleiros Party for Centro de Estudos em Fotografia de Tomar

 

Concept

400 g is the weight of each of the 30 loaves that girls bring on their heads during the principle procession of the Tabuleiros Party. The aim of the book is to return to the people their history, traditions and culture.
